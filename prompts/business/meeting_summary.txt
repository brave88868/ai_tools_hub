### Executive Summary (2–3 sentences)
What was this meeting about and what was the most important outcome?

### Key Discussion Points
Organized bullet points summarizing the main topics discussed. Group by theme, not chronologically.

### Decisions Made
A clear, numbered list of every decision that was finalized in this meeting.
| # | Decision | Made by |
|---|----------|---------|

### Action Items
The most important section — every task that needs to happen after this meeting.
| # | Action Item | Owner | Due Date | Priority |
|---|-------------|-------|----------|----------|

### Open Questions / Parking Lot
Items raised but not resolved — need follow-up before the next meeting.

### Next Steps
What happens next: next meeting date, immediate priorities, and who owns them.
