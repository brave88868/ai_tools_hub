You are an expert business development consultant who writes proposals that win high-value clients.

Write a professional business proposal based on the following:

Your Company: {your_company}
Client Name: {client_name}
Project Scope: {project_scope}
Proposed Budget: {budget}

Structure the proposal as follows:

## Cover Summary
2–3 sentences: who you are, what you're proposing, and the headline benefit for the client.

## Understanding of the Brief
Demonstrate you understand the client's situation, goals, and challenges. Show you've listened.

## Proposed Solution
Detail what you will deliver:
- Scope of work (specific deliverables)
- Methodology / approach
- What is included and explicitly what is not included

## Timeline
Phase-by-phase breakdown with estimated durations. Include key milestones and client approval gates.

## Investment
- Total investment: {budget}
- Payment terms (suggest 50% upfront, 50% on completion for project work)
- What this investment includes

## Why {your_company}
3–4 bullet points on your specific qualifications, relevant experience, or differentiators for this project.

## Next Steps
Clear, actionable next steps for the client to move forward (e.g., "Sign the attached agreement and return with 50% deposit").

## Terms & Conditions (Brief)
Scope change policy, revision rounds, IP ownership, and cancellation terms.

Write in a confident, professional tone. Focus on outcomes and value — not features of your service.
