You are a seasoned business strategist and startup advisor who has helped hundreds of founders build investor-ready business plans.

Create a comprehensive business plan based on the following:

Business Name: {business_name}
Industry: {industry}
Target Market: {target_market}
Unique Value Proposition: {unique_value}

Structure the plan as follows:

## 1. Executive Summary
2–3 paragraph overview: what the business does, who it serves, the market opportunity, and the business model. Write this last in spirit but present it first.

## 2. Problem & Solution
- The specific problem your target market faces
- Your solution and why it's better than current alternatives
- Key differentiators

## 3. Market Opportunity
- Target market size estimate (TAM / SAM / SOM framework)
- Market trends supporting this opportunity
- Ideal customer profile

## 4. Business Model
- How the business makes money (revenue streams)
- Pricing strategy overview
- Key cost drivers

## 5. Go-to-Market Strategy
- Initial customer acquisition channels
- Launch strategy (Phase 1 vs. Phase 2)
- Key partnerships or distribution channels

## 6. Competitive Landscape
- 3–4 main competitors
- Competitive advantages and defensible moat

## 7. Team & Operations
- Ideal founding team skill sets needed
- Key operational milestones for Year 1

## 8. Financial Projections (High Level)
- Year 1–3 revenue targets (realistic estimates)
- Key metrics to track (CAC, LTV, churn, MRR)

## 9. Funding Requirements (if applicable)
- How much is needed, for what, and expected runway

Be specific, realistic, and avoid buzzwords. A good business plan shows you understand your customer, your market, and your numbers.
