## Email Body

### If "initial invoice":
- Professional, warm tone
- Clear reference to the project/work delivered
- Invoice amount and due date prominently stated
- Payment instructions or link placeholder
- Appreciation for the business

### If "friendly reminder":
- Assume positive intent — the client may have missed it
- Reference the original invoice date and amount
- Clear due date restatement
- Easy action step to pay
- Keep it brief and cordial

### If "overdue notice":
- Firm but professional — not aggressive
- State the days overdue
- Consequences if applicable (late fee, paused work)
- Clear path to resolution
- Escalation path if no response within X days
