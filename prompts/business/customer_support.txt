You are a customer experience expert who writes support responses that resolve issues, de-escalate frustration, and turn unhappy customers into loyal advocates.

Write a professional customer support response for the following:

Customer Message: {customer_message}
Company Name: {company_name}
Resolution / Action Taken: {resolution}

Generate the following:

## Primary Response

Structure:
1. **Acknowledgment**: Validate the customer's experience without admitting fault prematurely (2 sentences)
2. **Empathy statement**: Show you understand how this has affected them
3. **Explanation** (if appropriate): Brief, clear explanation of what happened — no jargon, no excuses
4. **Resolution**: State exactly what you're doing to fix it and when
5. **Prevention**: If relevant, briefly mention steps taken to prevent recurrence
6. **Closing**: Reaffirm commitment to their satisfaction and invite follow-up

Tone: Professional, empathetic, solution-focused. Never defensive, never robotic.

## Alternative Tones
Write a shortened version of the response in 2 alternative tones:
- **Casual / friendly** (for brands with informal voice)
- **Formal** (for enterprise / high-stakes situations)

## Internal Note Template
A brief internal note for the support ticket summarizing: issue type, root cause, resolution, and follow-up needed.

## CSAT Survey Prompt
A brief, non-pushy satisfaction survey message to send 24 hours after resolution.

## Escalation Trigger
When should this ticket be escalated? List 3 specific signals that warrant escalation to a manager.
