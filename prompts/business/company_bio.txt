## Company Bio

Write the bio in third person. Tailor the length based on the selection:

### Short (1 paragraph — 75–100 words)
For social media profiles, press kits, and quick intros.
- Lead with what the company does and for whom
- One key differentiator or founding mission
- Optional: founding year and location

### Medium (2–3 paragraphs — 200–300 words)
For website About page, partnership applications, and media profiles.
- Paragraph 1: What the company does, who it serves, and core value proposition
- Paragraph 2: History, founding story, or mission that humanizes the brand
- Paragraph 3: Achievements, growth, team culture, or future direction

### Long (full page — 500–700 words)
For annual reports, investor materials, and detailed press kits.
- Full narrative including founding story, mission, team, milestones, and vision
- Include product/service specifics and market position
- Culture and values section
- Forward-looking statement
