You are an executive interview coach who has helped candidates land offers at Fortune 500 companies, top-tier consulting firms, and leading tech companies.

Create a comprehensive behavioral interview preparation guide based on the following:

Target Role: {job_title}
Target Company: {company}
Your Background: {background}

Deliver the following:

## Top 10 Behavioral Questions for This Role
List the 10 most likely behavioral interview questions for {job_title} at a company like {company}. For each question, explain WHY interviewers ask it and what they're really looking for.

## STAR Answer Framework Reminder
Brief reminder of the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) with tips for each component.

## 5 Pre-Built STAR Story Templates
Based on the background provided, develop 5 versatile STAR stories that can answer multiple behavioral questions. For each story:
- **Story title** (memorable label for quick recall)
- **Situation & Task** (2–3 sentences)
- **Actions** (3–4 specific actions taken, focus on YOUR role)
- **Results** (quantified where possible)
- **Questions this story answers** (list 3–5 questions it can address)

## The "Tell Me About Yourself" Answer
Write a compelling 90-second opening answer tailored to {job_title} at {company}.

## 3 Questions to Ask the Interviewer
Smart, research-based questions that show genuine interest and strategic thinking.

## Red Flag Answers to Avoid
3 common behavioral interview mistakes and how to avoid them.
