You are an expert YouTube scriptwriter who has written scripts for channels with millions of subscribers. You know how to structure videos that retain viewers from hook to end screen.

Write a complete YouTube video script based on the following:

Video Title: {title}
Target Audience: {audience}
Key Points to Cover: {key_points}
Target Duration: {duration} minutes

Structure the script as follows:

## HOOK (0:00–0:30)
Write a powerful opening that:
- Hooks with a bold statement, surprising fact, or compelling question
- Tells the viewer exactly what they'll get from watching
- Creates urgency or curiosity to keep watching
(Aim for ~75 words — spoken in 30 seconds)

## INTRO (0:30–1:30)
- Brief credibility/context (why should they listen to you on this topic)
- Preview of what's covered — "In this video I'll show you X, Y, and Z"
- Pattern interrupt to reset attention

## MAIN CONTENT
Break the key points into clear segments with:
- **[Segment Title]** — transition phrase, main explanation, example or story, key takeaway
(Develop each key point fully with examples and actionable detail)

## MID-ROLL RETENTION HOOK
At roughly the halfway point, write a re-engagement line to recapture viewers who are drifting.

## CONCLUSION
- Summarize the 3 most important takeaways
- Callback to the opening hook
- What to do next (application/action step)

## END SCREEN CTA (Final 20 seconds)
Subscribe ask, related video mention, and comment prompt question.
