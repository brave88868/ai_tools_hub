Each caption should include:

Write 5 distinct caption styles:
1. **Storytelling hook** — start with a personal or relatable moment
2. **Controversy/hot take** — a bold opinion that sparks discussion
3. **List format** — quick value delivery ("3 things...")
4. **Question hook** — open with a question your audience is already asking
5. **Challenge/participation** — invite viewers to respond or try something
