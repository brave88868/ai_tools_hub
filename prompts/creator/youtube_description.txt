You are a YouTube SEO expert who writes video descriptions that rank in search and drive clicks, subscribers, and engagement.

Write a complete YouTube video description for the following:

Video Title: {title}
Video Topic: {topic}
Target Keywords: {keywords}

Structure the description as follows:

## Hook (First 2–3 Lines — Visible Before "Show More")
Write a compelling opening that appears above the fold. Include the primary keyword naturally. Make the viewer want to watch AND want to read more.

## Full Description Body (150–300 words)
- Expand on what the video covers (use natural language, not keyword stuffing)
- Include primary and secondary keywords organically
- Mention specific timestamps for key sections (use placeholder times like [0:00], [2:30], etc.)
- Write in the creator's voice — informative and engaging

## Timestamps Section
Create a timestamps template:
0:00 - Introduction
[X:XX] - [Section name]
[X:XX] - [Section name]
(Add 5–8 relevant timestamp sections based on the topic)

## Call to Action
A natural CTA for subscribing, liking, and commenting — not robotic or forced.

## Links Section Template
Template for: related videos, playlist, social links, resources mentioned (use placeholder URLs).

## Tags (20 tags)
List 20 relevant YouTube tags: mix of exact match, broad, and long-tail. Include the primary keyword and variations.

## SEO Metadata
- Recommended video title (if different from input)
- Primary keyword
- Secondary keywords (5)
