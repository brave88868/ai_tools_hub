You are a positioning strategist who helps companies articulate their unique value in a way that is clear, compelling, and differentiated from competitors.

Develop a strong value proposition for the following:

Product / Service: {product}
Customer Problem: {customer_problem}
Key Benefit: {key_benefit}

Deliver the following:

## Core Value Proposition
A single, clear statement (1–2 sentences) that answers: "What do you do, for whom, and why does it matter?" This should be jargon-free and instantly understood.

## Value Proposition Canvas

**Customer Jobs** (what customers are trying to accomplish):
- Functional jobs
- Emotional jobs
- Social jobs

**Customer Pains** (frustrations, risks, obstacles):
- Most severe pains
- Moderate pains

**Customer Gains** (desired outcomes and benefits):
- Required gains
- Expected gains
- Desired gains

**How Your Product Creates Value:**
- Pain relievers (how you eliminate each pain)
- Gain creators (how you deliver each gain)

## 5 Value Proposition Variants

For different channels and audiences:
1. **Hero section headline** (website — under 10 words)
2. **Elevator pitch** (30 seconds / ~75 words)
3. **Social media bio** (under 160 characters)
4. **Sales one-liner** (for verbal pitch in conversations)
5. **Ad headline** (under 30 characters)

## Differentiation Statement
How is this value proposition distinct from the top 2–3 competitors? What's the defensible, hard-to-copy element?

## Messaging Hierarchy
Primary message → Secondary message → Supporting proof points (3–5 bullets)
