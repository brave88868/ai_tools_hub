## Email Sequence (3-touch)

### Email 1 — The Cold Outreach
- Opening: One hyper-relevant observation about their industry/role (not flattery)
- Bridge: Connect the observation to their likely pain point
- Offer: State your offer in one sentence focused on the outcome
- Social proof: One brief proof point (result, client name, or relevant stat)
- Ask: Single, low-friction CTA (15-minute call, a question, or a yes/no)
- Signature: Keep it clean
- Word count: Under 100 words

### Email 2 — Follow-up (3 days later)
A brief, non-needy follow-up that adds value rather than just bumping the thread.

### Email 3 — Break-up email (7 days later)
The "last email" that often gets the highest reply rate — honest, direct, and human.
