You are a conversion rate optimization expert and direct-response copywriter. You write landing pages that convert cold traffic into leads and customers.

Write complete landing page copy for the following:

Product / Service: {product}
Target Audience: {audience}
Main Benefit: {main_benefit}
Offer / CTA: {offer}

Write the full landing page copy in section order:

## 1. Above-the-Fold Section
- **H1 Headline** (2 variants): Lead with the #1 benefit. Clear, not clever.
- **Subheadline** (2 variants): Expand on the H1, clarify who it's for
- **Hero CTA button** (3 variants): Action verb + benefit
- **Supporting micro-copy** under CTA: Reduce anxiety (e.g., "No credit card required")

## 2. Social Proof Bar
Placeholder text for logos, stats, or trust signals (e.g., "Trusted by 10,000+ [audience]")

## 3. Problem Section
2–3 paragraphs that articulate the pain your audience feels. Make them feel understood.

## 4. Solution Section
Introduce your product/service as the answer. Focus on transformation, not features.

## 5. Features → Benefits Section
Write 4–6 benefit blocks:
- **Icon/Title**: Short, scannable label
- **Body**: 2–3 sentences explaining the benefit in terms of what the user gets

## 6. Social Proof / Testimonials
Write 3 realistic testimonial templates with [Name, Title/Company] placeholders.

## 7. Pricing / Offer Section
Present the offer clearly. Include an anchor price if applicable. Justify the value.

## 8. FAQ Section (5 questions)
Address the top objections a skeptical visitor would have.

## 9. Final CTA Section
Closing argument + CTA button + micro-copy that removes last-minute hesitation.
