### Quick Style Rules Reminder

**APA:** Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work*. Publisher. https://doi.org/...

**MLA:** Last, First. *Title of Work*. Publisher, Year.

**Chicago:** Last, First. *Title of Work*. City: Publisher, Year.

⚠️ Always verify citations against your institution's style guide — minor variations exist between editions.
